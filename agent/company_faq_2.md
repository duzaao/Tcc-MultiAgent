**FlightCo — Company Information**

**Description:**
FlightCo is a fictional airline created to demonstrate a customer service agent. We offer domestic and international flights, focusing on safety, punctuality, and excellent customer service.

**Foundation:**
Founded on March 15, 2008, FlightCo has been operating for over 15 years, serving both national and international customers.

**What we do:**

* Passenger transport (domestic and international)
* Ticket sales in economy, business, and premium classes
* Options for additional baggage and travel insurance
* 24/7 customer service via help center and email

**Purchase options:**

* Direct purchase on the website using credit or debit card
* Phone purchase with assistance
* Installment payments available according to the issuing bank’s policies
* Purchase through partner agencies and travel platforms

**Important services and policies:**

* **Check-in:** online (from 24 to 1 hour before departure) or at the airport counter
* **Baggage:** allowance varies by fare; excess subject to additional fees
* **Changes and refunds:** depend on the purchased fare; promotional fares may have restrictions
* **In case of delay or cancellation:** assistance policies (meals, accommodation, rebooking) follow local regulations and our internal policy

**What to do in case of flight delay:**

1. Check your flight status on our website or app
2. Visit the FlightCo counter at the airport for information and rebooking
3. If the delay is prolonged, you may be entitled to meals, accommodation, or reimbursement as per applicable law
4. Contact our support via phone or help center to report the issue and request compensation

**Website documentation (customer guidance):**

* **How to buy:** “Buy” section in the main menu with step-by-step guidance for flight selection, passenger information, and payment
* **How to change or cancel:** “My Reservations” section with instructions on modifications, applicable fees, and deadlines
* **Baggage policy:** dedicated page with tables by fare and route
* **Customer service and complaints:** form on the “Contact” page and communication channels via phone/email
